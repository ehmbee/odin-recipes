Tumeric Rice

Turmeric rice is a delicious and healthy side dish that can be served with a variety of main dishes such as curries, stews, grilled meats or vegetables. It's easy to make, budget-friendly and versatile, making it a great addition to mealtimes.

Ingredients

  • 200 g Basmati Rice
  • 1 teaspoon Turmeric
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Onion Salt optional
  • 550 ml Chicken Stock or use vegetable stock
  • 1/2 teaspoon Sea Salt

Directions

  1. Rinse the rice in cold water (place it in a sieve over the sink to do this) then put the rice in a large lidded saucepan with turmeric, garlic powder, onion salt (if using), and the bay leaf.
  2. Place on the hob and pour in the chicken stock. Bring to a boil.
  3. Immediately turn down the heat to low and place a lid on the saucepan.
  4. Cook for 12-14 minutes until the water has been absorbed. Fluff the grains up with a fork, season with salt, and serve!
